Facebook Messenger App Privacy Policy

This Application stores a unique ID based on your Facebook Messenger profile ID in its database. When you answer questions about language preferences and proposed translated placenames, that information is stored and connected to your profile.

No data about your Facebook profile, friends, or posts is stored by CityNamer / names.georeactor.com / Georeactor.

This Application does not post on Facebook for you. It is limited to Facebook Page messages and the Messenger App.

This Application does not store your OpenStreetMap account (unless you use a separate OpenStreetMap sign-in).
